Who we are?

LightStick.ph is a Philippine-based distributor of K-Pop music. Going beyond general music distribution, we hope to become a contact point between K-POP artists and fans around the world.

From Korea, Original Albums and Goods We handle 100% AUTHENTIC original LightStick and goods.

Products are distributed from Korea to worldwide with LightStick.ph certified fast and safe delivery.

Where are we located?


Our main branch is located in Quezon City, Metro Manila Philippines, but all of our products came from South Korea. We also have other branches located in Pasig, Mandaluyong and Makati City.

Our goal is to provide you with a seamless experience while looking for KPOP merch. That's why we came up with the idea of creating a one stop shop for all your KPOP needs.
